                             DEITIES DATABASE
=============================================================================

PRIMARY FACT STRUCTURE:
god(Name, Mythology, Gender, Status, Era)

PARAMETERS EXPLAINED:
- Name: The deity's name (atom)
- Mythology: The religious/mythological tradition (greek, norse, hindu, etc.)
- Gender: male, female, none, or neutral
- Status: supreme_deity, major_deity, minor_deity, primordial, prophet, avatar, divine_figure
- Era: ancient, classical, medieval, modern

STATUS HIERARCHY (highest to lowest):
1. supreme_deity - Top-level gods (Zeus, Odin, Brahma, etc.)
2. major_deity - Important gods with significant roles
3. minor_deity - Lesser gods with specific functions
4. primordial - Ancient primeval deities (Chronus, Izanagi, etc.)
5. prophet - Religious messengers and leaders
6. avatar - Divine incarnations (Rama, Krishna)
7. divine_figure - Religious figures with divine status
